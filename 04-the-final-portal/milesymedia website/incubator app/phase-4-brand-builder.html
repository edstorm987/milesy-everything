What we're doing in Brand Builder

Brand Builder turns the priorities surfaced in Diagnostics into a brand kit, a story, and your launch-ready assets.

Logo design and final art happen between you and the design team — this page tracks the artefacts you need to gather and the decisions you need to make.

📚 Brand lessons
Brand questionnaire

1. In one sentence — what is your business about?

2. Who is the person you want to attract? Be specific.

3. Three brands you admire (any industry). Why?

4. Three words your clients would use to describe working with you.

5. What feeling should the brand evoke in 0.5 seconds?

Answer privately first; share with the design team during your kick-off.

Colour & font picker (placeholder)

This section ships in the Brand Kit round (T4 R009). For now, jot your two starting colours + a body / display font pair into the BOS branding modal — those flow through to your portal.

Asset upload hints

Send these to the design team via your client portal:

• Logo files (SVG preferred; PNG with transparent background otherwise).

• 3–5 high-res lifestyle / hero photographs.

• Font files or Google Fonts links you want used.

• Any existing brand guidelines, palettes, or moodboards.
