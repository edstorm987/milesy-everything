🧮 How we got this number

The range above is derived from your answers, not from your live business data. Three inputs feed it:

  1. Headroom — how far each topic score sits below 100, averaged across the topics you answered.
  2. Confidence — what fraction of the 5 topics you actually answered. Less coverage → narrower claim.
  3. Sector benchmark multiplier — typical revenue impact a healthy version of each topic produces in your kind of business.

This is a self-report, not a measurement. The range is deliberately wide. A real audit replaces it with measured numbers.

🔌 What we'd verify against — for the real version

To convert this from a self-report to a measured audit, we'd plug into:

  • 🔍
    Google Search Console — actual impressions, clicks, ranking positions, queries you appear for.
  • 📍
    Google Business Profile Insights — direction requests, calls, map vs search appearances.
  • ⚡
    Lighthouse / PageSpeed Insights — real performance/SEO/accessibility score, LCP, CLS, INP.
  • 📈
    GA4 / server-side events — actual conversion rates, source attribution, drop-off pages.
  • 💷
    Stripe / QuickBooks — real revenue, MRR, refunds, customer LTV.
  • 🗺
    SERP rank tracker (DataForSEO / Ahrefs) — third-party verification of where you actually rank.
