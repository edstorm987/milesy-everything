Privacy

Plain-English summary of what we collect and why. The long-form policy is available on request — email hello@milesymedia.co.

What we collect

Email addresses you give us through the Health Check, the Resource Finder, signup, or contact forms. Anonymous usage analytics on the marketing site. Whatever you choose to enter into your portal once signed in.

Why

To run the things you asked us to run — score your Health Check, send your weekly digest, host your client portal. We do not sell or rent your data, ever.

Where it lives

On infrastructure we control (Vercel, Postgres). Backups are encrypted at rest. Your portal data stays scoped to your tenant — other Aqua customers never see it.

Your rights

Email hello@milesymedia.co to export, correct, or delete your data. We respond within 30 days. If you signed up to a mailing list, every email carries a one-click unsubscribe.
