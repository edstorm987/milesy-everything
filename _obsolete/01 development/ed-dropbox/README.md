# Ed's drop-box

Dump anything in here that you want me to look at or absorb. I'll find it on the next prompt.

## Folders

- `screenshots/` — Notion / current site / competitor screenshots. Drop PNG/JPG. Filenames don't matter.
- `notion-export/` — your full Notion export (Markdown & CSV preferred). I'll parse the structure and pull modules / page hierarchy / database columns into the BOS.
- `branding/` — logos, colour palettes, font files, brand guidelines, product photos. Anything I should match the look-and-feel against.

## How to share

For most things, just drag-and-drop into the relevant folder. I'll see them next time you message me.

For Notion specifically: open the page in Notion → `⋯` (top-right) → `Export` → format `Markdown & CSV` → unzip → drop the unzipped folder into `notion-export/`.

For huge files (>50MB) drop a Drive/Dropbox link in chat instead.

## Conventions

- I read the contents of this folder fresh on each turn — older files don't get auto-deleted, so feel free to leave reference material here long-term.
- If you want me to ignore a file, prefix it with `_`.
- If you want me to delete something, say so in chat — I won't touch files in here without permission.
